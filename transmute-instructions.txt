# Transmute Instructions

## Items needed (in sorted order)

2 Hearty Durian
4 Fleet-Lotus Seeds
3 Mighty Banana
2 Hearty Truffle
2 Chillshroom
4 Rushroom
2 Blue Nightshade
2 Bird Egg
2 Voltfin Trout
2 Diamond



# Steps

a. Equip multi-shot bow, shock arrow, Hylian Shield

b1. Drop on the ground ("special" pile):
	1 Shard of Dinraal's Horn
	1 Shard of Naydra's Horn
	1 Farosh's Scale
b2. Drop on the ground ("other" pile):
	7 other dragon parts

c1. Overload with 6 bows
c2. Overwrite bows in inventory
c3. Equip Radiant Shield -> shield jump

d1. Hold/sell
	1 Hearty Durian, 2 Mighty Banana, 1 Hearty Truffle, 1 Chillshroom
d2. Pick up
	1 other, 1 other, 1 Farosh's Scale, 1 other

e1. Hold/sell
	3 Fleet-Lotus Seeds, 1 Blue Nightshade, 1 Bird Egg
e2. Pick up
	1 other, 1 other, 1 Horn

f1. Hold/sell
	3 Rushroom, 1 Voltfin Trout, 1 Diamond
f2. Pick up
	1 other, 1 other, 1 Horn
